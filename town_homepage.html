About

Westhill is a small suburban town in Aberdeenshire with a somewhat large population of about 12,000, Making it one of the largest towns in Aberdeenshire. It offers a nice and quite day to day with some amzaing nature walks close by. Westhill also offers decent infostructure with a shoping center, larger super markets and an industrial district all with in the town. Westhill has a few hotels the main of which is located nice and close to said infostructure.Even though it is located in the shire it is only a short drive / bus journey to the city. All this means westhill would offer an ideal starting point to explore aberdeen with being stuck in the city or out in the middle of nowhere
