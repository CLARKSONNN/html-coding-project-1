About

Westhill is a small suburban town in Aberdeenshire with a large population of about 12,000, Making it one of the largest towns in Aberdeenshire. It offers a nice and quiet day-to-day with some amazing nature walks close by. Westhill also provides a decent infrastructure with a shopping centre, larger supermarkets and an industrial district in the town. Westhill has a few hotels the main of which is located nice and close to said infostructure. Even though it is located in the shire it is only a short drive/bus journey to the city. All this means Westhill would offer an ideal starting point to explore Aberdeen without being stuck in the city or out in the middle of nowhere

Map of Westhill

Over recent years Westhill has seen a large amount of growth due to the booming oil and gas industry which all of Aberdeen experienced. as well as loads of new housing being built. a large part of Westhills growth can be credited to the the expansion of its industrial district with large oil companies / financial expansion there.
