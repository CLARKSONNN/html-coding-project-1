activtes

There are many different places to visit around Westhill this web page will focus on the most prevalent of them. Westhill is more of a family-orientated town and the activities nicely reflect this while still allowing for something for everyone

Westhill pool

The Westhill swimming pool is a great place for kids and adults alike to play in the water or get some hardcore practice. Located near the centre of Westhill great place to waste away hours

Westhill golf club

The Westhill Golf Club is a good spot to tee up and try to beat the par. Located near the top of Westhill it would offer a nice day trip for any golf lovers

Wsthill library

The library is filled with great books and plenty of choices for the bookworms among us. Located near the centre of town and next to one of the major hotels would be an ideal place to pass the time.

westhill swiming pool

The Westhill swimming pool is a nice semi-modern swimming pool. With 6 lines and a full dynamic floor allowing for different depths. It offers fairly nice facilities and a normal range of sessions. However, in recent years it has seen a decline in availability due to restricted opening hours.
